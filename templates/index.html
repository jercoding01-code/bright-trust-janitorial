Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our janitorial and cleaning operations.

What cleaning services do you provide?

Bright Trust Janitorial Inc. offers comprehensive daily and weekly commercial office cleaning, residential home cleans, post-construction cleanup, Airbnb turnovers, floor maintenance, washroom sanitizing, dusting, disinfection, garbage removal, and window cleaning.

Are you fully insured and bonded?

Yes, we are fully insured and bonded. We carry comprehensive liability coverage to protect your premises, our employees, and your business assets during every cleaning session.

What regions do you service in Ontario?

We serve the entire Niagara Region and surrounding municipalities in Ontario, including Niagara Falls, St. Catharines, Welland, Port Colborne, Grimsby, Lincoln, and Hamilton.

How are your cleaning quotes calculated?

Our initial quotes are calculated as flat rates depending on the service category (e.g. $145 for home/Airbnb turnovers and starting at $35 for office layouts). The final quote can be tailored by admins based on property dimensions and scope requirements.

Can I schedule recurring cleans (Weekly/Monthly)?

Yes! We offer contract schedules for weekly services (2-3 visits/week) and monthly services. Weekly contracts start at $185/week for offices and $195/week for residential locations. Monthly contract pricing is customized depending on scale.

Do I need to be present during the clean?

No, you do not need to be present. Many clients coordinate keyless lockbox access or supply codes. All cleaners are fully background-checked, vetted, and supervised for maximum trust.

What is your response time for booking requests?

We respond to all online quote inquiries, dates requests, and booking submissions within 24 hours. Our phone support is open 24/7 for operational coverage.

How do you ensure cleaning quality consistency?

We implement a strict 4-step process. Each clean follows a customized plan check-list, and our managers conduct routine quality assurance inspections to maintain service consistency.
