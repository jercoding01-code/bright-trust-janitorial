Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers to common inquiries about our operations and services.

What cleaning services do you provide?

Bright Trust Janitorial Inc. provides comprehensive daily and weekly commercial office cleaning, residential home cleans, post-construction sanitization, Airbnb turnover cleaning, medical facility disinfecting, and custom floor care.

Are you fully insured and bonded?

Yes, we are a fully insured and bonded professional cleaning company, protecting your premises, business assets, and our team members during every cleaning visit.

What regions do you service in Ontario?

We proudly service the entire Niagara Region and surrounding cities including Niagara Falls, St. Catharines, Welland, Port Colborne, Grimsby, Lincoln, and Hamilton.

How are your cleaning quotes calculated?

Estimates are calculated based on selected service types and frequency. We offer flat baseline rates (such as $145 for home/Airbnb cleaning and starting at $35 for offices) that can be adjusted by admins for larger properties.

Can I schedule recurring cleans (Weekly/Monthly)?

Absolutely! We specialize in recurring contract schedules. Weekly contract services (2-3 visits/week) start at $185/week for offices and $195/week for residential homes, alongside customizable monthly contracts.

Do I need to be present during the clean?

No, you do not need to be present. Many of our commercial and residential clients provide secure keyless entry or lockbox access. All of our cleaners undergo strict background checks and vetting.

What is your response time for booking requests?

We respond to all online quote inquiries and booking submissions within 24 hours. Our support and service operations are available 24/7.

How do you ensure cleaning quality consistency?

Consistency is managed through our 4-step quality assurance model. Every cleaner follows a customized site checklist, and our managers conduct routine quality control inspections to ensure standards are met.
