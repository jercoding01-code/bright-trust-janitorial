2. Booking Management & Deposit Workflow

Bookings enter the system via public online quote requests (/book/) or manual admin entry (+ Add New Booking).

Booking Status Progression

  • NEW Initial quote submitted by a client online.
  • CONTACTED Management has contacted the client to discuss custom pricing or details.
  • SCHEDULED Booking is confirmed. The system automatically generates a 25% Square Canada deposit link and emails the quote confirmation to the client.
  • COMPLETED Cleaner has completed the service and uploaded "After" photos. A sequential invoice number (BTJ-YYYY-XXXXXX) is locked.
  • CANCELLED Booking is cancelled and calendar slot capacity is released.
Automatic Square Downpayment Integration: When you set a booking to SCHEDULED, our system automatically calls the Square Developer API (POST /v2/online-checkout/payment-links) to generate a 25% deposit button inside the confirmation email.

3. Cleaner Staff Management Subsystem

Manage your field cleaners under the Cleaners tab (/dashboard/cleaners/):

  • 2-Factor Phone + PIN Login: Cleaners log into /cleaner/login/ using their 10-digit Phone Number and 6-digit PIN.
  • Hashed PIN Security: PINs are encrypted with Django's make_password hash. Admins can reset PINs on the cleaner edit form without seeing plain-text keys.
  • Daily Availability: Set cleaners to Available, On Leave, or Out Sick. Booking forms filter cleaner dropdowns to list only Active & Available staff.
  • Schedule Conflict Lock: The system prevents assigning a cleaner to two overlapping scheduled jobs.
  • 30-Day Mobile Sessions: Cleaners log in once on their phones and stay signed in for 30 days.
  • Automated Email Alerts: Assigning a job emails the cleaner details and address automatically.
  • Mobile Privacy Shield: Cleaners see property address and customer requests, but payment totals and financial calculations are strictly hidden.

4. Financial Auditing & CRA Compliance

  • Sequential Invoice Numbering: Completed jobs automatically receive an immutable invoice number (e.g. BTJ-2026-000001).
  • Financial Snapshots: Once an invoice is generated, pre-tax subtotals, HST calculations, and tax rates are locked from modification.
  • Excel CSV Exporter: Download a full financial audit log CSV from /dashboard/audit/export/.

5. Administrator FAQs & Troubleshooting

Question Solution
How do I reset a cleaner's PIN? Go to Cleaners tab -> Click Edit Profile / PIN -> Type new 6-digit PIN in Reset PIN field -> Click Save Changes.
What if a cleaner calls in sick? Go to Cleaners tab -> Edit cleaner -> Set Availability Status to Out Sick -> Save. The cleaner will be excluded from new job dropdowns.
Why am I getting a slot conflict warning? Either company capacity (MAX_CONCURRENT_CREWS) is full or the assigned cleaner already has a booking during that 4-hour window.
How do I update tax rates or prices? Go to Pricing Settings -> Update Base Fee, Sqft Multiplier, or Tax Rate (e.g. 0.1300 for 13% Ontario HST) -> Save.
